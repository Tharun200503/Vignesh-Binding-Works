ABOUT US

We are one of the pioneers in manufacturing of school notebooks. We are in this business since 1994 and have an exclusive facility for manufacturing purpose. The note books manufactured at our facility are of very high quality and supplied at very reasonable and competitive prices. We supply to schools and colleges almost all over Tamil Nadu. We usually take orders well in advance during the month of December and start manufacturing the requirements. So that we can ensure supply of the notebooks to the respective schools and colleges well in time. The specialty of our notebooks is that it carries the logos and emblems of the school independently and with attractive lamination work. We manufacture all kinds of notebooks with different dimensions as needed by the independent schools like 4 lines, 2 lines, square notebooks, notebook for mathematics, Graphs, Exam Sheets and Record Note.
